Ingredients French Toast
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/8-1/4 cup sugar of choice
  • 1/2 cup of milk/
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • cinnamon to taste - we used about 1/4 tsp
    •      Steps Step 1
  • Combine the Milk and Eggs. Beat the eggs and then add the milk and whisk a little more
    •      Step 2
  • Add Sugar and Vanilla. Add whatever sugar you like, some cinnamon and a bit of vanilla.
    •      Step 3
  • Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.
  • Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides.
    •      Step 4
  • Serve with Easy Spiced Syrup (recipe follows), if desired & Enjoy.
