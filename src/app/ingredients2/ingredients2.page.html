« Back Ingredients Scrambled Eggs with Avocado Toast
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley
  • 1 Slice of Bread
  • Half an Avocado
    •      Steps Step 1
  • Wash your hands
  • In a mixing bowl crack open 2 eggs and a pinch of salt and a pinch of pepper. Mix for 1 minute
  • Next in a large non-stick pan add 2 teaspoons of oil over medium heat. Let heat up for 1 minute.
  • Next, pour the eggs into the pan. Keep moving the eggs until you see that the egg is on longer raw.
  • When your finish, turn off your stove
    •      Step 2
  • Put 1 slice of bread in a toaster
    • Step 3
  • Grab your avocado and cut it in half
    •      Step 4
  • Next Spread avocado on bread
  • Place your eggs and bread with avocado in a plate & Enjoy!
