Ingredients Ham Sandwich
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Ham 1-3 Slices
  • 4 slices of Tomato
  • 2 Slices of Yellow Cheese
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Mayonnaise
    •      Steps Step 1
  • Wash your hands
  • Grab two slices of bread then place your bread on a pan at medium heat.
  • Wait until you see light brown on your bread on both sides
  • Then add 1 slice of cheese for both of your bread
  • Next put a led on your pan so your cheese will melt.
    •      Step 2
  • Wash your hands and grab a cutting board then grab a tomato and cut 4 slices
  • Wash your romaine lettuce then cut off the root of the lettuce
  • Grab two 2-4 pieces of ham or as many as you want
    •      Step 3
  • Finally, assemble your ham sandwich.
  • Grab your bread with cheese and place it on a plate
  • Then add your chopped of tomatos, lettuce, & mayonnaise
    •      Step 4
  • Enjoy!
