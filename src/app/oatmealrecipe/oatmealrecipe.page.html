Ingredients Oatmeal
  • ½ cup of oats
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • (Otional)Banana, Blueberries, Strawberries, Peanut Butter
    •      Steps Step 1
  • Wash your hands
  • Get a pot and place it on the stove over medium heat
  • Add ½ a cup of milk and your 1 cup of oats and one teaspoon of cinnamon to the pot.
    •      Step 2
  • Next cook for 8-10 minutes over medium heat; stir to make sure it does not stick to the pot or pan.
  • When you finish, turn off the stove.
    • Step 3
  • (Optional) Cut up a banana into the size you want.
  • Finally get a bowl and add our oatmeal and then your banana mix everything in & Enjoy!
