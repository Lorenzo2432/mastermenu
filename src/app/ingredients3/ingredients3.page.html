Ingredients Chicken Alfredo
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic
  • ½ cup of heavy cream
  • ½ clove garlic crushed
  • ½ cup of parmesan cheese grated
  • 2 tablespoon of cut parsley
    •      Steps Step 1
  • Wash your hands and grab any pasta you have at home.
  • Place 1 cup of water in a pot and leave it untill it boilslet, then put your ½ cup of pasta in your boiling water and let it cook for 10-14 mi until your pasta is soft and easy to rip over medium heat.
  • Check your pasta every 5mi to see if it is soft.
  • Next grab a non-stick pan then add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil to your pan
    •      Step 2
  • Next, grab a cutting board then wash your hands then grab your chicken make sure to not wash your chicken at all!!
  • Cut up the amount of chicken you want on the cutting board.
  • Then add ½ teaspoon of salt and ½ teaspoon of black pepper and ½ teaspoon of rosemary and cut up garlic next add your seasoning to your chicken.
  • Next grab a non-stick pan then add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil to your pan
    •      Step 3
  • Cook your chicken until you see no pink chicken.
  • Cut a piece of the chicken and see if there's pink, if so keep it cooking,
  • If not, turn off the stove and let your chicken rest for 5 minutes
  • cut your chicken into long strips
    •      Step 4
  • Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat. Add cream and cook for 5 minutes,
  • Then add garlic and cheese and whisk quickly, heating through. Stir in parsley
  •      Step 5
  • Stir the drained pasta into the sauce.
  • Turn off the burner and put the cooked fettuccine into the pot with the sauce.
  • Use tongs or a spoon to toss the noodles so they're completely coated with the sauce.
  •      Step 6
  • Get a bowl and place your pasta than your chicken then your sauce
  • Enjoy!
