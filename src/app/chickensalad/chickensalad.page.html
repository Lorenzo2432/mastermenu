« Back Ingredients Chicken Avocado Salad
  • Breast Chicken
  • ½ cup of romaine lettuce
  • ½ of an avocado
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • ½ cup of spinach
  • ½ cup of carrot
    •      Steps Step 1
  • Wash your hands and wash your romaine lettuce and tomato and spinach and your carrot.
  • Dry your romaine lettuce and tomato and spinach and carrots.
    • Step 2
  • Grab a cutting board and start to cut up all your greens and carrots.
  • Cut off the end of the lettuce making sure to take out the white piece of the lettuce
  • Do the same with your carrots cutting off the ends and top.
  • With your spinach just take off a pit of the steam
  • Then cut your avocado in half and just use one half of the avocado.
    •      Step 3
  • Wash your hands and then grab a non-stick pan and set it over medium heat.
  • Next, add 3 spoons of cooking oil to your pan
    •      Step 4
  • Then add ½ teaspoon of salt, ½ teaspoon of black pepper, ½ teaspoon of rosemary, & ½ teaspoon of paprika to your chicken making sure everything is covered.
  • Cook your chicken until you see no pink chicken.
  • Cut a piece of your cooking chicken and see if there's pink chicken if so keep cooking until no pink chicken is seen
    •      Step 5
  • Grab a cutting board and cut the chicken into long strips
    •      Step 6
  • Grab a bowl and place your cut up greens on the button and add your chicken and avocado on the top.
  • Enjoy!
